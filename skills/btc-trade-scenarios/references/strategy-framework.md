# BTC Scenario Framework

Use these scenario labels when possible:

- `回踩做多`: Price returns into the long pullback zone and shows acceptance.
- `突破做多`: Price confirms above the long breakout trigger.
- `反压做空`: Price rejects inside the short rejection zone.
- `跌破做空`: Price confirms below the short breakdown trigger.
- `观望`: The structure is choppy, reward-to-risk is poor, or invalidation is unclear.

Preferred wording:

- `逻辑`: One short paragraph.
- `触发条件`: One sentence.
- `入场区/触发价`: Specific zone or trigger.
- `止损`: One price.
- `止盈1 / 止盈2`: Use nearby structure, not fantasy targets.
- `失效条件`: Explain what structural change breaks the thesis.
