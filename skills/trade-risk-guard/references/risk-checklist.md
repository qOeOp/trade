# Trade Risk Checklist

Before finalizing a BTC trading reply, verify:

1. The answer is framed as scenario analysis, not a promise.
2. The answer includes invalidation and risk language.
3. The answer does not prescribe extreme leverage or revenge trading.
4. The answer does not imply the assistant knows the future.
5. The answer does not replace the user's own execution, sizing, or suitability decisions.
